âž— Theoretical Framework (LaTeX)

The Quasi-Linear MOND (QUMOND) field equation relates the MONDian acceleration $\mathbf{g}$ to the Newtonian acceleration $\mathbf{g}_N$:

\[\mathbf{g} = \nu\left(\frac{|\mathbf{g}_N|}{a_0}\right) \mathbf{g}_1\]

The engine utilizes the **Simple Interpolation Function** to bridge the high-acceleration (Newtonian) and low-acceleration (Deep-MOND) regimes:

\[\nu(y) = \frac{1}{2} + \sqrt{\frac{1}{4} + \frac{1}{y}}\]

where $y = |\mathbf{g}_N| / a_0$ and $a_0 \approx 1.2 \times 10^{-10} \text{ m/s}^2$.
